## 7.1 Future Tense (भविष्यत् काल — Bhaviṣyat Kāl)

**Formation:** Stem + personal future endings (which encode gender, number, and person)

The future tense endings for Hindi are unique — they don't use a separate auxiliary like present/past tenses do.

**Full conjugation of करना (karnā — to do/make):**

| Subject | Masculine | Transliteration | Feminine | Transliteration |
|---------|-----------|-----------------|----------|-----------------|
| मैं | करूँगा | karūṃgā | करूँगी | karūṃgī |
| तू | करेगा | karegā | करेगी | karegī |
| तुम | करोगे | karoge | करोगी | karogī |
| वह / यह | करेगा | karegā | करेगी | karegī |
| हम | करेंगे | kareṃge | करेंगी | kareṃgī |
| आप | करेंगे | kareṃge | करेंगी | kareṃgī |
| वे / ये | करेंगे | kareṃge | करेंगी | kareṃgī |

**Pattern summary:**
- 1st person singular: stem + **-ūṃgā / -ūṃgī**
- 2nd person intimate: stem + **-egā / -egī**
- 2nd person familiar: stem + **-oge / -ogī**
- 3rd person / आप: stem + **-egā / -egī** (singular); stem + **-eṃge / -eṃgī** (plural)

**Examples:**
- मैं कल दिल्ली **जाऊँगा** → I will go to Delhi tomorrow (m.)
- वह परीक्षा में अच्छे अंक **लाएगी** → She will get good marks in the exam (f.)
- हम अगले साल हिंदी **सीखेंगे** → We will learn Hindi next year
- क्या तुम आओगे? → Will you come?

**Negative future:** Insert **नहीं** before the future verb:
- मैं नहीं **जाऊँगा** → I will not go
- वह वहाँ नहीं **जाएगी** → She will not go there

## 7.2 Imperative (आज्ञार्थ — Ājñārtha)

The imperative is used for commands, requests, and instructions.

| Register | Pronoun | Formation | Examples |
|----------|---------|-----------|---------|
| Intimate (rude/casual) | तू | Verb stem alone | कर! जा! खा! पढ़! बैठ! |
| Familiar | तुम | Stem + **-o** | करो! जाओ! खाओ! पढ़ो! बैठो! |
| Formal/Polite | आप | Stem + **-iye** or **-ījiye** | कीजिए! जाइए! खाइए! पढ़िए! |

**The verb करना (to do) in imperative:**

| Register | Form | Transliteration | English |
|----------|------|-----------------|---------|
| Intimate | कर | kar | Do! |
| Familiar | करो | karo | Do! (friendly) |
| Formal | कीजिए | kījiye | Please do (polite) |

**More examples:**

| Verb | Intimate (stem) | Familiar (-o) | Formal (-iye) |
|------|-----------------|---------------|---------------|
| जाना (to go) | जा | जाओ | जाइए |
| आना (to come) | आ | आओ | आइए |
| खाना (to eat) | खा | खाओ | खाइए |
| पीना (to drink) | पी | पीओ | पीजिए |
| बोलना (to speak) | बोल | बोलो | बोलिए |
| देखना (to see) | देख | देखो | देखिए |
| सुनना (to listen) | सुन | सुनो | सुनिए |
| बैठना (to sit) | बैठ | बैठो | बैठिए |

**Negative Imperative:** Use **मत** + stem (intimate/familiar):
- मत जाओ! → Don't go!
- मत करो! → Don't do it!
- मत रो! → Don't cry!
- वहाँ मत जाओ → Don't go there

> [!note] For formal negative imperatives, use **मत** + formal form or the subjunctive: जाइए मत / न जाएँ (please don't go, formal).

## 7.3 Subjunctive / Presumptive (संभावना)

**Formation:** Stem + **-e** (m.s./general) / **-eṃ** (pl./formal)

The subjunctive expresses:
- Possibility or uncertainty
- Polite suggestion
- Purpose/intention (with ताकि)

**Examples:**
- शायद वह **आए** → Perhaps he will come (uncertainty)
- शायद बारिश **हो** → Perhaps it will rain
- क्या मैं अंदर **आऊँ**? → May I come in? (polite request)
- ताकि हम समझ **सकें** → So that we can understand

## 7.4 Modal Verbs (सहायक क्रियाएँ)

### सकना (saknā — can / be able to)

**Formation:** Main verb stem + **सकना** (conjugated)

| Subject | Positive | Negative |
|---------|---------|---------|
| मैं (m.) | कर **सकता हूँ** | नहीं कर **सकता** |
| मैं (f.) | कर **सकती हूँ** | नहीं कर **सकती** |
| वह (m.) | कर **सकता है** | नहीं कर **सकता** |
| हम | कर **सकते हैं** | नहीं कर **सकते** |

**Examples:**
- मैं हिंदी बोल **सकता हूँ** → I can speak Hindi (m.)
- वह तैर **सकती है** → She can swim
- क्या आप यहाँ आ **सकते हैं**? → Can you come here? (formal)
- मुझे नहीं पता, मैं बता नहीं **सकता** → I don't know, I cannot tell

---

### चाहना (cāhnā — want to / wish to)

**Formation:** Main verb **infinitive** + **चाहना** (conjugated for subject)

| Subject | Example | Translation |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| मैं (m.) | जाना **चाहता हूँ** | I want to go |
| मैं (f.) | जाना **चाहती हूँ** | I want to go |
| वह (m.) | खाना **चाहता है** | He wants to eat |
| वह (f.) | पढ़ना **चाहती है** | She wants to study |
| हम | घर जाना **चाहते हैं** | We want to go home |

---

### चाहिए (cāhie — should / ought to / need)

**Formation:** Subject (oblique) + **को** + verb infinitive + **चाहिए**

चाहिए does not change form — it is invariable.

| Hindi | Transliteration | English |
|-------|-----------------|---------|
| मुझे जाना चाहिए | mujhe jānā cāhie | I should go |
| उसे पढ़ना चाहिए | use paṛhnā cāhie | He/she should study |
| हमें खाना खाना चाहिए | hameṃ khānā khānā cāhie | We should eat food |
| आपको आराम करना चाहिए | āpko ārām karnā cāhie | You should rest |

---

### पड़ना (paṛnā — have to / must / be compelled to)

**Formation:** Subject (oblique) + **को** + verb infinitive + **पड़ना** (conjugated)

| Hindi | Transliteration | English |
|-------|-----------------|---------|
| मुझे जाना पड़ता है | mujhe jānā paṛtā hai | I have to go (habitually) |
| मुझे जाना पड़ा | mujhe jānā paṛā | I had to go (past) |
| मुझे जाना पड़ेगा | mujhe jānā paṛegā | I will have to go (future) |
| उसे काम करना पड़ता है | use kām karnā paṛtā hai | He/she has to work |

> 💡 **Tip:** The difference between चाहिए and पड़ना:
> - **चाहिए** = should (moral obligation, advice): तुम्हें सच बोलना चाहिए (You should tell the truth)
> - **पड़ना** = have to (compelled by circumstances): मुझे आज काम करना पड़ेगा (I'll have to work today — whether I like it or not)

## 7.5 Passive Voice (कर्मवाच्य — Karma-vācya)

**Formation:** Object + **से / के द्वारा** (by agent) + past participle + **जाना** (conjugated)

| Active | Passive |
|--------|---------|
| राम ने चिट्ठी लिखी (Ram wrote the letter) | राम के द्वारा चिट्ठी लिखी गई (The letter was written by Ram) |
| सरकार ने नियम बनाए (Government made rules) | सरकार द्वारा नियम बनाए गए (Rules were made by the government) |

> [!note] Passive voice is less common in everyday Hindi than in English. In formal/written Hindi and newspaper language it appears frequently. For IGCSE, focus on recognising it in reading tasks.

## 7.6 Compound Verbs / Vector Verbs (संयुक्त क्रिया)

Hindi uses a second "light verb" after the main verb to add a subtle meaning — aspect, direction, or beneficiary. The main verb takes the stem form (or past participle), and the light verb is conjugated.

| Light Verb | Name | Added Meaning | Example |
|-----------|------|---------------|---------|
| **जाना** (jānā) | Completive | Action completed, often irreversible | खा **जाना** = eat up (completely) |
| **देना** (denā) | Benefactive (other) | Doing for someone else's benefit | बता **देना** = tell/inform (for them) |
| **लेना** (lenā) | Reflexive | Doing for oneself | खा **लेना** = eat for oneself; सोच **लेना** = decide/think through |
| **आना** (ānā) | Directional (toward) | Coming here to do something | चला **आना** = come (over here) |
| **डालना** (ḍālnā) | Forceful completion | Done deliberately / with force | कर **डालना** = just go ahead and do it |
| **पड़ना** (paṛnā) | Sudden/unexpected | Something happening suddenly | रो **पड़ना** = burst out crying |
| **उठना** (uṭhnā) | Sudden start | Sudden beginning of action | बोल **उठना** = suddenly speak out |

**Examples:**
- मैंने सारा खाना खा **लिया** → I ate up all the food (for myself)
- उसने मुझे बता **दिया** → He told me (did it for me)
- वह अचानक रो **पड़ी** → She suddenly burst into tears
- जल्दी खा **लो** → Eat up quickly (for yourself, while you can)

## 7.7 Key Definitions

| Term | Hindi | Transliteration | Definition |
|------|-------|-----------------|------------|
| Future tense | भविष्यत् काल | bhaviṣyat kāl | Actions that will happen |
| Imperative | आज्ञार्थ | ājñārtha | Command / instruction form |
| Subjunctive | संभाव्य | saṃbhāvya | Possibility / polite suggestion |
| Modal verb | सहायक क्रिया | sahāyak kriyā | Helping verb (can, should, must) |
| सकना | saknā | can, be able to | Ability modal |
| चाहिए | cāhie | should, need to | Obligation modal (invariable) |
| पड़ना | paṛnā | have to, must | Compulsion modal |
| Passive voice | कर्मवाच्य | karmavācya | Subject receives the action |
| Compound verb | संयुक्त क्रिया | saṃyukta kriyā | Main verb + light vector verb |
| Vector verb | प्रेरणार्थक | — | Light verb adding aspectual meaning |
