## 1️⃣ Numbers (संख्याएँ — Saṃkhyāeṃ)

## 📅 Days of the Week (सप्ताह के दिन — Saptāh ke Din)

| Hindi | Transliteration | English | Etymology |
|-------|-----------------|---------|-----------|
| सोमवार | Somvār | Monday | Moon day (सोम = moon) |
| मंगलवार | Maṃgalvār | Tuesday | Mars day (मंगल = Mars) |
| बुधवार | Budhvār | Wednesday | Mercury day (बुध = Mercury) |
| गुरुवार | Guruvār | Thursday | Jupiter day (गुरु = Jupiter/teacher) |
| शुक्रवार | Śukravār | Friday | Venus day (शुक्र = Venus) |
| शनिवार | Śanivār | Saturday | Saturn day (शनि = Saturn) |
| रविवार | Ravivār | Sunday | Sun day (रवि = sun) |

> 💡 **Tip:** Hindi days are named after the same celestial bodies as English/French/Spanish days — just using Sanskrit names. गुरुवार is also commonly called **बृहस्पतिवार** (Bṛhaspativār).
