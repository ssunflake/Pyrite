## 18.1 Names and Formulae of Organic Compounds

> **Organic chemistry** is the chemistry of carbon-containing compounds (excluding CO, COв‚‚, carbonates, and a few other exceptions).

### Why Carbon Forms So Many Compounds
- Carbon can form **4 covalent bonds**
- Can bond to itself in **long chains, branched chains, and rings**
- Can form single, double, and triple bonds
- Result: millions of organic compounds

### Naming System (IUPAC)

Organic names are built from:
1. **Root** = number of carbon atoms in the longest chain
2. **Suffix** = functional group
3. **Prefixes/numbers** = branches and positions

| Carbon atoms | Root |
|-------------|------|
| 1 | meth- |
| 2 | eth- |
| 3 | prop- |
| 4 | but- |
| 5 | pent- |
| 6 | hex- |
