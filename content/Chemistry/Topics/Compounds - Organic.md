## 🔬 Key Reactions Summary

| Reaction | Reactants | Product | Condition |
|----------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Combustion | Alkane + O₂ | CO₂ + H₂O | Heat |
| Substitution | Alkane + Cl₂ | Chloroalkane + HCl | UV light |
| Addition (Br₂) | Alkene + Br₂ | Dibromoalkane | Room temp |
| Hydrogenation | Alkene + H₂ | Alkane | Ni catalyst, 150°C |
| Hydration | Ethene + H₂O | Ethanol | H₃PO₄, 300°C, 60 atm |
| Fermentation | Glucose | Ethanol + CO₂ | Yeast, ~30°C, anaerobic |
| Esterification | Acid + Alcohol | Ester + H₂O | Conc. H₂SO₄, warm |
| Dehydration | Ethanol | Ethene + H₂O | Al₂O₃, 180°C |
| Cracking | Long alkane | Short alkane + alkene | High T / catalyst |
