## 10.2 Opinions About School Subjects

### Opinion Structures

| French structure | English |
|-----------------|---------|
| J'adore ... | I love ... |
| J'aime (beaucoup) ... | I (really) like ... |
| Je n'aime pas (trop) ... | I don't (really) like ... |
| Je déteste ... | I hate ... |
| Je trouve ... + adj | I find ... |
| ... m'intéresse / m'ennuie | ... interests / bores me |
| ... est ma matière préférée | ... is my favourite subject |
| Je suis fort(e) en ... | I am good at ... |
| Je suis nul(le) en ... | I am terrible at ... |

### Reasons and Justifications

| French | English |
|--------|---------|
| parce que c'est intéressant(e) | because it is interesting |
| parce que c'est utile | because it is useful |
| parce que c'est facile / difficile | because it is easy / difficult |
| parce que le/la prof est sympa | because the teacher is nice |
| parce que j'aime les chiffres | because I like numbers |
| parce que ça m'aide à comprendre le monde | because it helps me understand the world |
| parce que je veux travailler dans... | because I want to work in... |
| je ne comprends pas grand-chose | I don't understand much |

**Sample sentences:**
- *J'adore les sciences parce que les expériences sont fascinantes.* — I love science because the experiments are fascinating.
- *Je trouve les maths difficiles, mais mon prof est très patient.* — I find maths difficult, but my teacher is very patient.

**Typical school day description:**
*Les cours commencent à huit heures. On a une récré de dix minutes à dix heures. On déjeune à la cantine à midi. Les cours finissent à dix-sept heures.*
(Lessons start at eight. We have a ten-minute break at ten. We have lunch in the canteen at midday. Lessons finish at five.)

> [!note] In France, *collège* = lower secondary (Year 7–10), NOT the same as British "college". A *lycée* is equivalent to Year 11–13 in the UK. Don't confuse with English *college*!
