## 13.9 Opinion Phrases About the Environment (with Subjunctive)

| French | English |
|--------|---------|
| *Il est essentiel **que nous réduisions** notre empreinte carbone.* | It is essential that we reduce our carbon footprint. |
| *Il faut **que les gouvernements agissent** maintenant.* | Governments must act now. |
| *Je suis convaincu(e) que la technologie **peut** nous aider.* | I am convinced that technology can help us. |
| *Il est dommage **que les gens ne recyclent pas** davantage.* | It is a pity that people don't recycle more. |
| *Bien **que** la situation soit grave, il y a des solutions.* | Although the situation is serious, there are solutions. |
| *À moins **que** nous ne changions nos habitudes, le problème s'aggravera.* | Unless we change our habits, the problem will worsen. |

> 💡 **Exam tip:** Using the subjunctive in environmental essays is an excellent way to show grammatical range. Phrases like *il faut que...*, *il est essentiel que...*, and *bien que...* are high-scoring structures.
