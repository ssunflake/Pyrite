# 07. Formulas & Quick Reference — Perimeter, Area and Volume

[[07. Notes - Perimeter, Area and Volume|← Notes]] | [[00. Maths Index|Index]]

---

## 2D Shapes

| Shape | Perimeter | Area |
|-------|-----------|------|
| Rectangle | 2(l + w) | lw |
| Triangle | a + b + c | ½bh |
| Parallelogram | 2(a + b) | bh |
| Trapezium | a + b + c + d | ½(a+b)h |
| Circle | 2πr | πr² |
| Sector | (θ/360)×2πr + 2r | (θ/360)×πr² |
| Kite / Rhombus | sum of sides | ½d₁d₂ |

```
Arc length    =  (θ/360) × 2πr
Sector area   =  (θ/360) × πr²
Segment area  =  sector area − ½r²sin(θ)
Triangle area (SAS) = ½ab·sin(C)
```

---

## 3D Solids

| Solid | Surface Area | Volume |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Cuboid | 2(lw+lh+wh) | lwh |
| Cylinder | 2πr²+2πrh | πr²h |
| Cone | πrl+πr² | ⅓πr²h |
| Sphere | 4πr² | (4/3)πr³ |
| Hemisphere | 3πr² | (2/3)πr³ |
| Prism (any) | 2×cross-section + lateral | cross-section area × l |
| Pyramid | base + ½×perimeter×l | ⅓×base area×h |

```
Cone slant height:  l = √(r² + h²)
```

---

## Similar Shapes (scale factor k)

```
Lengths / perimeters  →  × k
Areas                 →  × k²
Volumes               →  × k³
```

---

## Unit Conversions

```
Length:   1 m = 100 cm = 1000 mm
Area:     1 m² = 10 000 cm²;   1 cm² = 100 mm²
Volume:   1 m³ = 10⁶ cm³;      1 litre = 1000 cm³ = 1000 ml
```
