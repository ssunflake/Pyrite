## Ratio

```
Simplify:       divide all parts by HCF
Share in a:b:   total parts = a+b;  each part = total ÷ (a+b)
Fraction of a:  a / (a+b)
```

---

## Proportion Types

| Type | Symbol | Equation | Graph |
|------|--------|----------|-------|
| Direct (x) | y ∝ x | y = kx | Straight line through origin |
| Direct (x²) | y ∝ x² | y = kx² | Parabola through origin |
| Direct (√x) | y ∝ √x | y = k√x | Square root curve |
| Inverse (x) | y ∝ 1/x | y = k/x | Hyperbola |
| Inverse (x²) | y ∝ 1/x² | y = k/x² | Steeper hyperbola |

## Finding k

```
1. Write y = k·f(x)
2. Substitute known (x, y) pair
3. Solve for k
4. Re-write full equation
5. Substitute new x to find y
```

---

## Unitary Method

```
Direct:   find value for 1 unit → multiply by n
Inverse:  find total (x × y = const) → divide by new x
```
