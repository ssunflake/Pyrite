## 🔺 Fleming's Rules

| Rule | Hand | Used for |
|------|------|---------|
| **Left-hand rule** | Left | Motor effect — direction of force on conductor |
| **Right-hand rule** | Right | Generator — direction of induced current |

> **Left Hand:** First finger = Field, SeCond finger = Current, thuMb = Motion  
> **Right Hand:** First finger = Field, thuMb = Motion, seCond = Current
